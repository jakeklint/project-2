Activities

CHESS

Sharpen your strategic thinking and enjoy friendly competition in our Chess Club. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting, this club offers a supportive environment to learn, play, and challenge your peers while improving your critical thinking skills

Aerobics class

Stay fit, energized, and stress-free by joining the Aerobics Club. With dynamic routines and a focus on wellness, this club is perfect for anyone looking to build stamina, enhance coordination, and foster a healthy lifestyle in a fun group setting.

Literary Lecture

Dive into the world of ideas and imagination with our engaging Literary Lectures. Featuring renowned speakers and thought-provoking topics, these sessions are designed to inspire creativity, broaden horizons, and spark intellectual discussions.

Donation

Make a difference by participating in our donation drives. These initiatives provide an opportunity for students to give back to the community, foster compassion, and support those in need through collective action.

Literary Art

Express your creativity and explore the beauty of words in our Literary Art activities. From poetry and prose to storytelling and scripts, this platform encourages students to share their unique voices and perspectives.

Dance Club

Unleash your rhythm and passion for movement with the Dance Club. Whether you love hip-hop, classical, or contemporary styles, this club offers a stage to learn, perform, and connect with fellow dance enthusiasts.

Concerts

Experience the joy of music and performance at our school concerts. These events bring the university community together to celebrate talent, teamwork, and the power of artistic expression.

Graduation

Celebrate your academic achievements and the start of a new chapter at our memorable graduation ceremonies. This milestone event honors your hard work, growth, and success alongside family, friends, and peers.
